Vagabond Tattoo Studio was established in 2012 on East London’s popular Hackney Road. Our focus is on clean and original tattoos, spanning a range of styles. We custom design all our own artwork and ensure that every tattoo is solid whilst being executed with a high level of precision. Our artists aim is to interpret your ideas into something unique that will stand the test of time. Our clean welcoming studio will also allow for a great relaxed experience. Resident artists include Paul Hill, Andrew Hulbert, Rose Harley, Adam Ruff, Juanjo Rangel (Jay) Leti Mortimer and Anika Lukins as well as regular international guest artists. For enquiries pop into the studio and have a chat with a member of the team. Alternatively check out our enquiries page for booking options and we will get back to you as soon as we can. We look forward to meeting you and hearing your ideas.
